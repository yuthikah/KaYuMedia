About Us

A Passion for Storytelling Through the Lens

KaYu Media is a Durban-based photography and videography service dedicated to capturing life's most meaningful moments. Founded with a shared desire to preserve special occasions through powerful imagery and motion, we deliver an experience that is relaxed, professional, and memorable from start to finish.

Our Vision

To be a trusted media brand known for capturing authentic moments families and businesses will cherish for years.

Our Mission

To preserve meaningful moments, celebrate life's special occasions, and create lasting visual memories.
